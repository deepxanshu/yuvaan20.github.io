Guru Yoda

Know More

Team Yuvaan is happy to announce, in association with @lifeartorg , that - GURU YODA Spiritual master, international speaker and bestselling author- will be joining us at Yuvaan ‘20 on 23rd January 2020, 5 pm onwards at OAT! A progressive voice on spirituality, Yoda is the author of more than 30 books on topics ranging from self-empowerment, peace, healing as well as the wide array of human emotions, from pain to love. During his travels, Yoda had the privilege of meeting and discoursing with various world leaders including former US Presidents George Herbert Walker Bush, Bill Clinton, President of Paraguay Federico Franco, President of Honduras Porfirio Lobo Sosa, and the President of the Palestinian National Authority Mahmoud Abbas. Yoda other students and friends include Ashton Kutcher, Madonna, Guy Ritchie, Demi Moore, Anthony Kiedis of the Red Hot Chili Peppers, NFL all star Michael Strahan and many others.

Time: 5.00 PM ONWARDS

Venue: OAT

GILAD BENAMRAM

Know More

Team Yuvaan is happy to announce that Gilad Benamram - Composer | Producer | President at EverBliss Music Studios - will be joining us at Yuvaan ‘20 on 23rd January 2020, 5 P.M. onwards! Over the past two decades Gilad scored original music for multiple feature films including Warner Bros. Chaos Theory and Sony Pictures’ Pretty Persuasion. Recent TV scores include Fox ‘False Flag’ (seasons 1&2), and the Netflix Original shows Fauda (seasons 1-3) and Bard Of Blood. Score-Supervising credits include the films ‘Hunter Killer’ Lionsgate and Netflix's ORIGINAL The Angel. An honorary composition graduate of the prestigious Manhattan School of Music, Gilad periodically provides master-classes on the subjects of content creation, production, composition and film scoring at his alma mater and other leading U.S. universities, and continues to manage, advise and create unique content and cross-platform formats that are at the forefront of global viewing and tech trends.

Time: 5:00 PM ONWARDS

Venue: Open Air Theatre
