Dr. Rahat Indori

Mehfil-e-Sama

Know More

आँख में पानी रखो, होठों पर चिंगारी रखो, जिंदा रहना है तो तरकीबें बहुत सारी रखो। यकीन हो के ना हो, बात तो यकीन की है, हमारे जिस्म की मिट्टी इसी जमीन की है। A Painter turned Professor, Poet and then Hindi film Lyricist, Rahat Indori is known for his lovely splendor and supreme duty to work. He was a Pedagogist of Urdu literature in Indore University before his talent was appreciated by the listeners of Urdu poetry across the World. All the urdu literature and poetry enthusiasts are invited to YLFF 2020. To listen to his mesmerizing poetry catch him on 24th Jan, Delhi Technological University

Time: 5:30 PM ONWARDS

Venue: Open Air Theatre
