Yuvaan '18

A place to experience something out-of-the-box and absolutely magical- the perfect ‘Elixir of Life’. This is exactly what we witnessed at the second edition of Yuvaan. With a footfall of over 40K, attendance by 150 colleges of great repute, a variety of events for all kinds of audiences, and graced with the presence of personalities like Durjoy Dutta, Yahya Bootwala, Simar Singh, Diksha Bijlani, Poshak Dua and the Nizami Brothers, Yuvaan’18 turned out as the biggest Youth Literature Fest in Northern India. Celebrating the power of words and providing a platform for the youth to express their thoughts, Yuvaan’18 was a dream come true for literary enthusiasts.

Yuvaan '17

Yuvaan - the literary fest of DTU - made an opulent entry with a grand first edition in the year 2017. The literary fest had a magnificent listing of guests like Major General GD Bakshi, Major Gaurav Arya, Rohit Sardana, Sunetra Choudhary, Talish Ray and Kishore Dayani. Celebrating arts and acting as a medium to express and to let go, Yuvaan 2017 was a haven for all literary enthusiasts; the very idea of Yuvaan was groundbreaking and so were those beautiful couple of days. With a footfall of over 20K, Yuvaan'17 was a success in every sense, and was drawing fame and praise in literary circles.
