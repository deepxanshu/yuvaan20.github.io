About YLFF'20

Literature & Film Festival of DTU

"A hero can be anyone. Even a man doing something as simple and reassuring as putting a coat around a young boy’s shoulders to let him know that the world hasn’t ended.” -Bruce Wayne 💯 . Everyone wants recognition for what they do, for the mark they leave upon this world- everyone wants to be a hero. But more often than not, many end up leaving scars, and not marks. In a world which is superficially progressing towards utopia but is being torn apart from the inside out, there still exist some people- people who serve as the beacon of hope in dark times. People who work towards uplifting others. People who transform from ordinary beings to superheroes. 💜 . After a semester slogging over exams, we present to you an experience to kick start your new year as well as the new decade with our very own Literary and Film Festival- Yuvaan 2020. This year at Yuvaan, we present to you an event enriched by the likes of such superheroes. This year we celebrate the true spirit of heroism and chivalry that shields us from all evil. This year, we celebrate “Eden de Übermensch”. 💫 . After three successful editions, we bring to you an invigorating fourth one. Get a taste of the best of all fields- indulge yourself in mesmerizing poetry, tickle your funny bones with some of the finest comedians, and get a chance to meet your superheroes. Participate in events with amazing rewards, dance away your worries at our mind-blowing concerts, and feast like never before at the legendary food stalls. Experience all of this and then some at Yuvaan 2020! ❣
